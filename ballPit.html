Animation and Hierarchical Transformations

Coded in Personal or Class Year Completed
TypeScript Class: CSCI4611 Programming Interactive Computer Graphics and Games 2023

Using 3D vectors and base code, I created a small game where you go and collect balls with a moving pit. The balls can interact with each other and are contained within a box.

This wasn't my first time working with small scales physics, I had made a small pinball game in Processing before, but this was my first time working in a 3D environment. The transition wasn't extremely difficult, and it was a good chance to begin working with objects in a 3D environment.
